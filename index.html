Clay × CanvasBoard
GTM Strategy Brief

Fixing CanvasBoard's GTM Motion

Messy data, unused product analytics, and a lack of standarized outreach playbook is hurting CanvasBoard bottomline.

~2,400 employees PLG + Enterprise motion 10M contacts 6-tool stack
01 — CanvasBoard GTM Pain

Three GTM Painpoints

PAIN 01
Data Hygiene & Trustworthiness
5M of 10M contacts are stale. Multiple enrichment vendors have created competing "sources of truth" — reps can't trust the data they act on.
PAIN 02
PLG Effectiveness Gap
Despite being captured in Amplitude, PLG is not being used by reps to target high value sign ups, leading to low conversion rates and wasted outreach.
PAIN 03
No Rep Standardization
Bombora delivers intent scores but reps have no playbook for what to do next. Outreach is manual, inconsistent, and untargeted at scale.
02 — Negative Business Impact from Current State

The Cost of Inaction

💸
Missed PLG Revenue
High intent free trial users churn when Sales doesn't intercept at the right moment. Each missed conversion is lost ARR.
🗑️
No ROI on Costly Data Vendors
Paying for multiple data vendors with no consolidation layer means duplicated spend, conflicting records, and zero single source of truth.
🎯
Reps Chasing the Wrong Leads
Without a scoring model, reps burn time on cold or low intent contacts while high quality leads go untouched. Outreach volume ≠ quality.
🔄
No Product Feedback Loop
PLG signals and Gong call insights never reach the PDE. CanvasBoard is built without real-time signal from Sales or users.
03 — How Can Clay Help?

Clay Becomes the Orchestration Layer

Single Source of Truth
Clay aggregates and consolidates enrichment across all data vendors, creating a reliable contact list for reps to use.
PLG Signal Scoring
Import Amplitude usage events for PLG into Clay. Build a product led score that identifies which signups are high value vs junk traffic.
Intent-Driven Outreach
Automate outreach using Bombora intent scores while keep reps in the loop with Slack notifications and Salesforce updates
Capture Product Feedback
Surface structured product feedback from Gong call transcripts and sync insights to Snowflake — giving PDE a continuous, real-time feedback loop.

05 — Priority Ranking for CanvasBoard

Where to Start and Why

1
Optimize PLG Conversion Rate
CanvasBoard already has PLG data in Amplitude. Clay connects PLG signals to a rep visible scoring model so the highest value free trial users get contacted immediately. This is the fastest path to incremental revenue without adding headcount.
Immediate revenue increase
2
Automate Outreach & Standardize Sales Process
Connect Bombora intent scores to Clay, triggering outreach sequences to prospects and notify reps via Slack alerts. Reduce time consuming manual updates in Salesforce by automatically pushing user information ensuring records stay up to date and the proper territories are assigned. Reps stop guessing and start executing a consistent, data-driven motion. High-value contacts get priority; low intent leads don't burn cycles.
Increased rep productivity, reduced time-to-outreach
3
Data Consolidation and Coordination
Aggregate, enrich, and filter contact data across all vendors — layering in Amplitude product analytics and qualitative signal from Gong call transcripts. Sync the unified dataset to Snowflake so teams like PDE have continuous, real time visibility without querying five different sources.
Reduced vendor spend + PDE feedback loop unlocked
